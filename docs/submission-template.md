## Team Members

## Problem

Engineering onboarding is currently driven by a manually maintained spreadsheet with static tabs for welcome copy, checklists, tools, channels, rituals, docs, and starter tasks. It takes managers hours to personalize, drifts quickly, and gives new hires a passive artifact instead of a guided experience.

## Solution

Spark is a Slack-based onboarding companion for new Webflow engineers. It turns onboarding into a stateful journey with:

- personalized Day 1 guidance
- team and codebase orientation
- static onboarding curriculum pulled from the spreadsheet and encoded as structured product data
- blocker triage and doc routing
- real contribution task discovery from the codebase
- a dry-run or live draft PR flow for early contributions
